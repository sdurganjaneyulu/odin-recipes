Fish Curry

Description

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish pieces
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes or tamarind pulp
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Curry leaves (optional)
  • Coconut milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Clean and cut the fish into pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, then cook until fragrant.
  4. Add tomatoes or tamarind pulp and cook until soft.
  5. Mix in turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
  6. Add water to form a curry base and bring it to a boil.
  7. Gently add the fish pieces and cook until tender.
  8. Add coconut milk if desired and simmer for a few minutes.
  9. Garnish with curry leaves and serve hot with rice.
